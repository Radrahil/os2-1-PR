# Instructions

1. Open an issue to request that FizzBuzz be implemented in the language of your choice.
2. Take a look at open issues and choose which one you want to do. You can choose your own issue too.
3. Fork and clone the repository, implement FizzBuzz in your language of choice, and make a pull request!

> [NOTE]: Make sure the implementation is called `<language>.<ext>`, e.g., `python.py` and is put in the `fizzbuzz` folder.
